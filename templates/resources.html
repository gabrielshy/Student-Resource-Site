StillHiring.today

StillHiring.today is a community-sourced list of tech companies that are hiring today. This resource is extremely helpful for those struggling to look for jobs in the tech industry!

Indeed

LinkedIn

Linkedin is an extremely helpful connection tool. It's the largest professional network which allows you to connecting with employers, while being able to showcase your capabilities and personality through your profile. It's highly recommended you create one before starting your professional careers!

Take Me There!

Handshake

Handshake is a great place to find jobs as a college student or graduate. They personalize job recommendations based on your information, which is helpful for finding the internship/co-op/job that's right for you!

Take Me There!

JOPWELL

JOPWELL is a career platform designed specifically for Black, Latinx, and Native American students and professionals. Here, internship and job opportunities are opened up for minorities looking to start or extend their careers!

Take Me There!

FlexJobs

FlexJobs is a great place to find remote jobs. This job-board is like any other, however, they mainly focus on flexible and remote jobs. One downside to the site is that it's a paid subscription, though it maintains it's high ratings!

Z-Library

Need textbooks for free? Z-Library is the world's largest ebook library, with almost every textbook available at your disposal. If you don't need a hard-copy, just come here!

Library Genesis

Library Genesis is an file-sharing based library which hosts scholarly journals, academic books, and way more. Here, students can find textbooks for classes and articles for projects!

SlugBooks

Need a hard-copy textbook? SlugBooks is a textbook search engine with access to online stores such as Amazon, AbeBooks, eCampus Buy, Textbooks.com and more!
